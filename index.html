I’m a hardworking and creative first year prebusiness student. I’m interested in marketing and have held various marketing positions throughout the past two years.

Since fall quarter I have been the RHA marketing assistant where I’ve become knowledgable on how to efficiently advertise residential programs, create flyers, take captivating photography and do marketing campaigns for Norm events. Through my position in RHA, I also developed a passion for helping the student body and being able to put my peers’ desires and needs into actual change.

Since Fall quarter, I have also been on the Alumni, Marketing and Public Relations Committee for UCR’s Camp Kesem. Camp Kesem is a nonprofit organization that is dedicated to supporting children impacted by a parent's cancer. On the AMP committee I further developed my ability to design flyers and to advertise club meetings and events. Additionally, this position required me to be able to work on a team and brainstorm in a group environment. Camp Kesem in general is something I’m extremely passionate about. I’ve been a part of Kesem since I was 8 years old and worked as a Counselor in Training last summer through UCSD’s Kesem chapter.

I also held the Public Relations position in my high school’s marching band which was what really sparked my passion for marketing and advertising. Through this position I documented the program’s practices, performances and competitions through photography and videography, designed flyers and ran the social media page. Additionally, I was dedicated to the marching program for all 4 years of high school where I developed strong dedication, organizational skills and work ethic.
